Business

Daniel is originally from and still currently residing within Toronto, Daniel is a full-time realtor with the Toronto Real Estate Board since 2014 with a focus on lead generation & conversion. Still servicing his clients, Daniel has recently partnered with the #14 ranked Re/Max team in Canada working as the team leader. Duties include 1 on 1 coaching, group seminars, Tom Ferry group masterminding, and an ongoing strategic focus to navigate the market resulting in a quarterly increase in units sold. 

As a coach who works full-time in the industry, my clients appreciate that I am in the trenches with them, adjusting & adapting to the markets, the uncertainties, and all other factors that determine if our clients will buy or sell.

Coaching

With my experience coaching individuals on a large-scale team, I’ve learned that everyone requires a different set of standards, something unique to their goals and their lifestyle. Very, very seldom have I found that two different agents required the same set of communication, coaching, and accountability strategies. For these reasons, I offer a variety of real estate coaching services to help you reach your goals and succeed in our industry. 

Accountability Plus+

The backbone of any successful business owner is accountability. Within our career, freedom comes easy however the accountability to produce for not only yourself, but everyone around you, is the hardest aspect of the business to master. Within the Accountability Plus+ program we will use our systems to plan your business for the year, breaking it down to quarterly goals and getting after it! 

1 on 1 Coaching Programs

Our one-on-one coaching sessions are tailored to your specific needs and goals. We'll work together to develop a customized plan to help you achieve success in your real estate career. Accountability Plus+ program is already integrated into our one-on-one program.

30-60-90

Our comprehensive 30-60-90 day training programs cover everything you need to know to succeed in the real estate industry. From the basics of real estate transactions, to advanced marketing strategies, this program has got you covered. There are various group training sessions, masterminds, and more included in this program. The 30-60-90 program is only open to full-time agents as it is a demanding program that requires at least 16 weeks.

No matter what stage you're at in your real estate career, we have a coaching program that's right for you. Use my Calendly link to book a call & let's talk about what your path to success looks like.
