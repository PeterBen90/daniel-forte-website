Meet Daniel

Daniel is someone who has literally built his real estate career from the ground up. With an extensive background in renovation and construction, he brings a remarkable insight that goes beyond traditional market analysis. It’s Daniel’s experience in so many aspects of real estate that allows him to take a holistic approach when helping clients.

Prior to obtaining his license, this enterprising realtor managed a series of construction projects, including demolitions and new builds. His educational background in Business Communications at Brock and York universities is also what laid the foundation for Daniel’s entrepreneurial drive.

Clients have always valued his efforts to keep things realistic throughout the entire process. Daniel does this by dedicating time each day to observing real estate trends, and then presenting the facts in a pragmatic way that encourages clients to make the best decisions.

In Daniel’s spare time, you’ll find him diving into the latest research in the areas of finance and technology.“Owning real estate is a huge accomplishment, especially in the GTA. My greatest satisfaction comes from helping clients achieve financial freedom through the incredible opportunities that open up for Canadian homeowners.”.
